FEATURES ENGINEERED (26 TOTAL)
================================================================================

1. TEMPORAL (5 features)
   • transaction_hour (0-23)
   • transaction_day (1-31)
   • transaction_month (1-12)
   • transaction_dayofweek (0-6)
   • transaction_dayofyear (1-366)

2. AMOUNT (5 features)
   • amount (raw value)
   • amount_log (log transformation)
   • amount_is_negative (refund flag)
   • amount_z_score (statistical deviation)
   • amount_vs_customer_avg (ratio to average)

3. CARD VELOCITY (6 features)
   • card_amount_mean (avg transaction)
   • card_amount_std (std deviation)
   • card_amount_min (minimum)
   • card_amount_max (maximum)
   • card_txn_count (total transactions)
   • card_unique_days (usage pattern)

4. MERCHANT RISK (2 features)
   • merchant_fraud_rate (fraud percentage)
   • merchant_txn_count (transaction volume)

5. GEOGRAPHIC RISK (2 features)
   • state_fraud_rate (region fraud rate)
   • state_txn_count (regional volume)

6. CUSTOMER PROFILE (4 features)
   • current_age (customer age)
   • credit_score (credit rating)
   • yearly_income (financial profile)
   • customer_fraud_rate (fraud history)

7. TRANSACTION TYPE (1 feature)
   • use_chip (online vs swipe)
